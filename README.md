# Angelite

**Luxury, redefined in cloth.**

Angelite is a bespoke e-commerce house dedicated to the luxury abaya — pieces designed for the woman who treats modesty as an art form and refinement as a daily ritual.

---

## Our Philosophy

We believe luxury is quiet. It does not announce itself; it is recognized. Every Angelite piece is built around a single conviction: that elegance lies in restraint, and that the finest garments speak through their fabric, their fall, and the care behind their making — not through ornament.

---

## Brand Identity

Our visual world draws from the textures of natural stone — soft, sunlit, and timeless.

**Signature Palette**
- **Angelite Blue** — our signature hue
- **Sand & Warm Beige** — grounding and inviting
- **Ivory** — pure and luminous
- **Taupe & Deep Beige** — quiet sophistication
- **Stone & Grey** — calm and considered

**Design Language**
Minimalist layouts. Elegant typography. Generous whitespace. Subtle borders that frame rather than interrupt.

---

## The Experience

### For Our Clients

A storefront designed to feel less like a shop and more like a private atelier. Clients can:

- Browse a thoughtfully curated catalog with refined filtering and sorting
- Explore each piece in detail, with full size guidance and complementary add-ons
- Personalize orders with notes for our team
- Save their cart and return to it across visits and devices
- Check out seamlessly with Apple Pay, card, or cash on delivery
- Track orders, revisit past purchases, and manage their profile from a private account
- Reach our customer care team directly via WhatsApp

The full experience is offered in both **English and Arabic**, with prices presented in the client's preferred currency.

### For Our Team

A private dashboard gives our team complete oversight of the house:

- A daily view of sales, recent orders, and performance
- Full product management, including sizes, add-ons, and bilingual content
- Hierarchical category organization
- Inventory tracking with low-stock alerts
- End-to-end order management, from confirmation through to refund
- Client profiles and purchase histories
- Promotional codes and seasonal campaigns
- Region- and category-aware tax configuration
